Top Wedding Destinations

We will be going over three of the top locations for destination weddings in recent years. These include Italy, Greece, and the South of France. We will be talking about venues, styles in those cultures, common themes, and common foods that are provided in these destinations. We'll also be talking about what makes these locations so enticing for people to get married there, and roughly how much each place costs.
