South of France


Finally, we will discuss Southern France, which is also commonly known as the South of France, or in French it is known as le midi. Southern France isn't one of the first places you think of when you hear "destination wedding," but it is beautiful and rich in history nonetheless. It's unique and rich in culture, as well. According to Chateau Bee Selection, there are a few popular venues in Southern France. Those would be the vineyards in Provence, Dordogne's countryside, or the beaches in the French Rivera. Southern France also has more mediterranian cuisine than Italy or Greece would offer. There's also many Chateaus, or castles, in France that you can get married at with lots of history behind them all, due to the historic royalty that once ruled France.
